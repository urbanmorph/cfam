What is ?

Pedalಶಾಲೆ is a Certified Bicycle Training Program where participants undergo a training that prepares them to properly participate in Indian traffic as a cyclist. Its main mission is to enable you to confidently handle your bicycle in city roads. This program is to promote active mobility in Bangalore and get more cyclists on the roads. A completion certificate rounds up the accomplishment of the participant.

Vision

Instilling confidence in people so they can make a choice of bicycling and prepare the participants to confidently pedal on Indian streets

Why are we doing this?

Our goal is to create enthusiasm and confidence amongst people by teaching how to safely handle the bicycle.

  • Creating perspective - Educate the public on rights and responsibilities.
  • Beginning with the notion that children are the country's future and tomorrow's citizens will assist to develop that worldview.
  • Promote women's participation in society by introducing cycling to the economically underprivileged areas of the population.

How are we doing this?

  • Improve their cycling skills.
  • Increase the participants' knowledge of traffic rules.
  • Popularise the idea of cycling.
  • Improve their health by using non-motorized transport.
  • Have every participant trained by a certified/professional instructor or trainer

Participant

Do you wish to cycle confidently on our roads? Click here to get trained by certified trainers at your nearest available location and recieve a certificate of accomplishment on completion of the training. You will be trained for a minimum of 10 hours by certified trainers and will undergo a road worthiness assment along with learning the rules of the road as a cyclist.

Trainer

Trainers undergo rigorous training and achieve certifcation from experienced instructors from Bangalore Bicycling school. Click here to become a certified trainer. We are on the lookout for women trainers a majority of our participants are women and children. You should know how to ride a bicycle confidently and be prepared to undergo a certification of minimum 6 hours for the same.

Instructor

Select group or master trainers from Bangalore Bicycling School are called instructors and they oversee the program and its contents. They have been training people in Bengaluru for the past 8 years and have established methodologies which they bring to this program.

About

This initiative is under an MoU between Karnataka Non-Motorised Transport Agency (KNMTA), Karnataka Bicycle Dealers Association(KBDA) and Citizens for SUstainability (CiFoS). The target is to train as many people as possible in Karnataka to learn to cycle confidently on Indian streets. The training program will be run across the city near stores of KBDA members.
