## ğŸ†˜ Troubleshooting

Common issues:

**No one is joining:**
- Promote Discord link more actively
- Add to website prominently
- Share in social media regularly

**Low engagement:**
- Seed discussions yourself
- Ask questions
- Create events and challenges
- Recognize and thank active members

**Spam/raiders:**
- Use AutoMod
- Increase verification level temporarily
- Ban raiders quickly
- Enable "Pause Invites" during raids

**Channel confusion:**
- Post channel guide in #welcome-start-here
- Use channel topics/descriptions
- Moderate off-topic posts gently
