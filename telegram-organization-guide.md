## ğŸ’¡ TELEGRAM-SPECIFIC BEST PRACTICES

### Why Indians Love Telegram:
- Low data usage
- Works on 2G/3G
- Better group management than WhatsApp
- No phone number sharing in groups
- Larger file sizes (2GB vs WhatsApp's 100MB)
- Better for organizing movements/communities

### Content Tips:
- Use emojis strategically (high engagement)
- Keep posts concise (mobile screens)
- Use hashtags for discoverability
- Post between 7-9 AM and 6-9 PM (peak commute)
- Mix text, images, and videos
- Always include clear CTAs

### Language:
- Primarily English for main channel
- Allow Hindi/regional languages in city groups
- Use simple, accessible language
- Avoid jargon (or explain it)
