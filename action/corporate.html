Hejje Gala: Bengaluru's Corporate Active Mobility Challenge

A city-wide competition encouraging corporates to promote walking and cycling among employees

What is Hejje Gala?

Hejje Gala is Bengaluru's flagship corporate active mobility challenge, bringing together leading companies to compete in promoting sustainable commuting among their workforce.

Partners: GBA, WRI India, AltMo, Walkaluru

How It Works:

  1. Register your company for the challenge
  2. Encourage employees to download the AltMo app
  3. Track activities (walking, cycling to work or transit stations)
  4. Compete on leaderboards against other companies
  5. Win recognition as Bengaluru's most sustainable employer

Support the National Active Mobility Bill

How corporate advocacy accelerates policy change

Why Corporate Support Matters

Government listens when business speaks. Your endorsement carries weight.

  • Policy Influence: Corporate letters to ministers are taken seriously
  • Budget Priority: Employer demand justifies infrastructure spending
  • Implementation Speed: Private-sector expertise accelerates project delivery
  • Public-Private Partnerships: Co-fund infrastructure near your offices

What You Can Do

  1. Sign Corporate Endorsement Letter to Ministry of Road Transport & Highways
  2. Join Industry Coalitions (CII, FICCI working groups on sustainable mobility)
  3. Public Statements in support of the bill from CEO/CSO
  4. CSR Commitments for infrastructure funding post-bill passage
  5. Employee Testimonials on need for safe cycling routes
