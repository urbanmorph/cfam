Indian Cities Active Mobility Infrastructure Database

14 cities • 13 variables • Updated Feb 2026

Comprehensive data on cycling and pedestrian infrastructure across India's major cities including:

  • Infrastructure metrics: Cycling lane length, protected lane coverage, footpath length, IRC:11 compliance
  • Usage data: Modal share for walking and cycling
  • Safety data: Annual road deaths, vulnerable user fatalities
  • Investment analysis: Required investment, ROI ratio, projected lives saved

Measurement technique: Field surveys, municipal data analysis, GIS mapping, IRC standards assessment

Cities covered: Bangalore, Mumbai, Delhi, Chennai, Hyderabad, Pune, Ahmedabad, Kolkata, Surat, Jaipur, Lucknow, Kanpur, Nagpur, Indore

Hejje Gala Corporate Challenge Statistics

882 companies • 9,156 users • Updated Feb 2026

Real-time participation and impact data from India's largest corporate active mobility challenge:

  • Participation: 882 companies, 9,156 active users, 2,12,339 activities tracked
  • Environmental impact: 509.95 tonnes CO₂ offset
  • Top companies: Qualcomm (35% participation), Novo Nordisk (50,000 km annually)
  • Mode breakdown: 67% cycling, 28% walking, 5% running
  • Health benefits: 85.3 million calories burned, ₹4.57 crore healthcare savings

Source: hejjegala.in • Platform: Altmo app GPS tracking

Data Usage Guidelines

License

All datasets are licensed under Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0). You are free to share and adapt the data for any purpose, including commercially, as long as you provide attribution.

Attribution

Please cite as:

Council for Active Mobility (2026). [Dataset Name]. Retrieved from https://cfam.in/data/

Updates

Datasets are updated quarterly (Indian Cities Infrastructure) or in real-time (Hejje Gala). Check the dateModified field in JSON files for last update timestamp.

Contact

Questions or data requests? Email data@cfam.in or join our Discord community.

Looking for API Access?

We're developing a REST API for programmatic access to CFAM data. Contact us to join the beta program.
