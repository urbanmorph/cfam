Meet our Active Mobility Councillors.

The council will work towards three important mobility modes that determine the future of sustainable cities.

Walking

We need to walk just as fishes need to swim or birds need to fly. A city that prioritises public spaces has a soul.

Bicycling

A safe connected bicycle route shows that the city treats a citizen on a bicycle as important as one in a car.

Public Transport

A developed city is not one where the poor can use cars but where even the rich use public transport.
