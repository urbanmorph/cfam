What is the Pedalಶಾಲೆ (Pedal Shaale)?

Or Certified Bicycle Training Program, is a form of prospect validation scheme where participants undergo a training that prepares them to properly participate in Indian traffic as a cyclist. Its main mission is to confidently handle your bicycle in city traffic. This program is to support active mobility in Bangalore and to give the confidence for all kinds of cycling in the future. A completion certificate rounds the accomplishment of the participant.

Vision

Instilling confidence in people so they can make a choice of bicycling and prepare the participants to confidently pedal on Indian streets

Why are we doing this?

Our goal is to create enthusiasm and confidence amongst people by teaching how to safely handle the bicycle.

  • Creating perspective - Educate the public on rights and responsibilities.
  • Beginning with the notion that children are the country's future and tomorrow's citizens will assist to develop that worldview.
  • Promote women's participation in society by introducing cycling to the economically underprivileged areas of the population. By shortening their commutes and providing them the freedom to travel wherever they like, cycling will empower women.

How are we doing this?

  • Improve their cycling skills.
  • Increase the participants' knowledge of traffic rules.
  • Popularise the idea of cycling.
  • Improve their health by using non-motorized transport.
  • Have every participant trained by a certified/professional instructor or trainer
